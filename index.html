Welcome to Free Bird Cafe

A Cafe with a Cause

A 100% Social Enterprise Cafe funding the Thai Freedom House which provides education for Burmese Refugees in Northern Thailand. At Free Bird we believe in creating a healthy and safe environment all the way around. Our vegetarian and vegan food is only made from the highest quality and fresh ingredients. While most food in Thailand is made from palm oils and sugar - you will only find coconut oil and goodies sweetened with coconut flower syrup.
