Welcome to Free Bird Cafe

A 100% Social Enterprise Cafe funding the Thai Freedom House which provides education for Burmese Refugees in Northern Thailand.

We provide vocational training for our students, to earn funds for Thai Freedom House, to nourish the Chiang Mai community by producing excellent quality food and drinks at Free Bird Cafe, and to be the face of Thai Freedom House in Chiang Mai, raising awareness about our work there.
